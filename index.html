Hymn to Jordan

StoryTravelers / Vimeo
We went on a 10 day road trip with friends and had the feeling having been there once before. A magical journey of recognition through smiles, stunning vistas, deep emotions, colors and tastes .. the mystery of Jordan. To honour this beautiful country we decided to create a Visual Hymn to Jordan.

Hi there! We’re a tribe of visual storytelling artists with a shared passion for genuine travel. We love to dive deep into travel experiences and tell about it through cutting edge videos and imagery. We create sparks that ignite your desire to travel the world.. maybe even forever
